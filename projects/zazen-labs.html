Zazen Labs is a product studio that operates at the intersection of focused discipline and modern technology. Based in Jacksonville Beach, Florida, the lab functions as an incubator for internal ventures, specializing in the end-to-end creation of digital products—from initial strategy and design to deployment and long-term maintenance.

Our Philosophy The name "Zazen" refers to seated meditation, a nod to the studio's approach of intentional, centered development. Rather than chasing every trend, the lab focuses on building functional, high-utility products with a deep emphasis on "Vibes," artificial intelligence, and user-centric design.

Active Labs Zazen Labs currently manages a diverse portfolio of active experiments and platforms, including:

  • PhotoBop: A simplified platform for collective photo sharing among communities and families.

  • Surfy: A highly personalized forecasting tool designed to help surfers align their schedules with their ideal ocean conditions.

  • Congress Machine: A transparency-focused dashboard that streamlines the process of tracking federal legislation and congressional activity.

Capabilities The studio leverages a specialized technical stack to bring concepts to life, with core expertise in:

  • Machine Learning & AI: Integrating intelligent automation into everyday tools.

  • XR/VR/AR: Exploring the boundaries of extended reality and immersive interfaces.

  • Full-Cycle Development: Handling the rigorous "Ship and Maintain" phase of the product lifecycle.

Zazen Labs is a space where technical expertise in web and mobile architecture meets a methodical, "ground-up" approach to venture building.
